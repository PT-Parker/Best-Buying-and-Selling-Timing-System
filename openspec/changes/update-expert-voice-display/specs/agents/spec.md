## MODIFIED Requirements
### Requirement: Dynamic Expert Selection Reasoning
The system SHALL display three expert personas with scores and present the winner rationale as a first-person statement from the top-scoring expert.

#### Scenario: Winner speaks
- **WHEN** expert scoring completes,
- **THEN** the UI shows bull/bear/neutral personas with their scores,
- **AND** the highest-scoring expert speaks the winning rationale in first-person.
